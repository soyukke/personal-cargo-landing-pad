# Personal Cargo Landing Pad

Personal Cargo Landing Pad gives cooperative multiplayer players their own
planet-side delivery destination.

- one owned cargo landing pad per player per surface
- explicit player ownership for space platforms
- cargo from a claimed platform routes to its owner's pad
- safe vanilla fallback when no matching pad exists
- ownership controls appear beside the vanilla landing pad and platform hub GUI
- existing saves are supported through manual claiming

Requires Space Age and **All the Landing Pads 1.0.0** for Factorio 2.0.
